How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, we rely on what’s publicly available: thousands of owner reviews across major retailers, long-term feedback on furniture forums, and published evaluations from reputable home-product reviewers. We also look for recurring complaints about power port failure, drawer alignment, and finish durability, these are the problems that actually drive owners crazy a year later. Our picks are the models that consistently score well on real-world livability: charging that still works after six months, drawers that don’t stick, and assembly instructions that don’t require a therapist. We avoid any product with a pattern of early electronic failures, no matter how good it looks in photos.
