Quick answer

For a budget nightstand that nails the basics and adds USB charging without breaking the bank, the VASAGLE Nightstand with Charging Station is our top pick thanks to its solid construction and popular industrial style.

If you prefer a cleaner, white look with a built-in electrical outlet, the Leick Home is the editor’s pick; the Walker Edison is a runner-up that offers a black finish and a roomy drawer at a very low price.

A nightstand with a built-in charging station is one of those small upgrades that makes a big daily difference. Instead of hunting for an outlet on the floor or using a separate bulky power strip, you get a tidy spot to dock your phone, tablet, or e-reader right beside the bed. For budget shoppers, that convenience often comes at a reasonable price, but not every affordable model is built to last or sized well for a bedside setup. When choosing the best budget nightstand with charging, look for a design that fits your bedroom’s style and your sleep habits. Consider the number and type of USB ports and outlets, the height relative to your mattress, and whether you need drawer or shelf storage. Durability matters too, particleboard can warp over time, so a solid build is key even at a lower price point. We’ve researched owner feedback and expert reviews to pick three models that balance cost, function, and looks.
