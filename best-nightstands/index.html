Quick answer

A nightstand is more than just a place to set your phone and a lamp, it’s the anchor of your bedside routine. With built-in charging stations becoming standard, today’s best nightstands double as cable-management hubs, letting you ditch the tangle of adapters and plug directly into the furniture. The key is balancing storage, size, and charging capability without sacrificing style or stability. When shopping, focus on drawer count (one deep drawer vs. two shallow ones), surface area for your lamp and glass of water, and the location of the charging ports, ideally they should be accessible but not in the way of the drawer. Also consider assembly difficulty: many flat-pack nightstands take 30–60 minutes to build. We’ve researched owner feedback and expert reviews across dozens of models to find the five that do it all right.
